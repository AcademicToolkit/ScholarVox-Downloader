This extension automates the export of the ScholarVox/Cyberlibris online book viewer.
It only runs on a small and clearly defined set of domains directly related to the service,
including a few institutional proxy domains required for universities to access the viewer.

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
All processing happens locally in the user's browser.
No network requests are modified, blocked, or redirected.

The extension does not bypass authentication and only works after the user has legitimately logged in.
Host permissions are limited to the minimum required for content script injection.

A full technical audit and detailed permission justification are available in docs/audit.md.
